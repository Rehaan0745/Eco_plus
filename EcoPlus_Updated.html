The Closed-Loop Ecosystem

Phase 1: Bio-Based Sourcing

Renewable corn-based feedstock is processed into biodegradable material.

Phase 2: Polymer Processing

Plant starch is converted into PLA and compounded for manufacturing.

Phase 3: Functional Products

Automated production lines manufacture durable packaging solutions.

Phase 4: Responsible Disposal

Products are designed for composting and environmentally responsible disposal.

Phase 5: Biodegradation

Under industrial composting conditions, the material biodegrades into biomass, water and carbon dioxide.
